Welcome Admin!

Welcome to the Admin Dashboard. As an admin, you have access to various features and functionalities that allow you to manage users, courses, and quizzes. Here's what you can do:

  • Register new students and teachers.
  • Enroll teachers in courses.
  • Create new courses.
  • Create quizzes for courses.

Use the navigation menu on the left to access different sections of the admin dashboard and perform these actions. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team.
